Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal data. Your photos are analyzed and deleted within 1 hour. We don't sell your data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Photos: When you scan a product, we temporarily process the image to extract ingredients. Photos are automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour after analysis.

Analysis History: Your scan history is stored locally on your device only. We do not sync or backup this data to our servers.

Preferences: Your madhab (school of thought) selection and language preferences are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze product ingredients and provide halal/haram classification
  • To improve our ingredient database and AI accuracy
  • To provide customer support when you contact us

3. AI-Powered Analysis

This app uses artificial intelligence (OpenAI GPT-4 Vision) to extract and analyze ingredient lists from product photos. The AI helps us:

  • Read ingredient text in multiple languages
  • Identify E-numbers and additives
  • Classify unknown ingredients

AI classifications for unknown ingredients are marked with lower confidence scores. We recommend verifying critical decisions with a qualified Islamic scholar.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for:

  • Improving our ingredient classification database
  • Academic research on food ingredients

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For image analysis (photos are processed according to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements to free users (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions

6. Advertisements

Free users see advertisements. We have configured our ad network to block inappropriate categories including alcohol, gambling, and dating apps. Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

7. Data Retention

  • Photos: Deleted within 1 hour of analysis
  • Local History: Stored until you clear app data or uninstall
  • Error Reports: Retained for 90 days for debugging

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your local scan history (in app settings)
  • Request deletion of any data we may have
  • Opt out of analytics
  • Access information about what data we have

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email if you've provided one.
