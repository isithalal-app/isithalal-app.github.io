Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Is It Halal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Images

When you use our app to analyze food products, we temporarily process images of ingredient labels. These images are:

  • Used only for ingredient extraction and analysis
  • Automatically deleted within 1 hour of processing
  • Not stored permanently on our servers
  • Not shared with third parties

2.2 Device Information

We may collect device language preferences to provide localized content. We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or personal information.

2.3 Analytics

We use anonymous analytics to improve our service. This includes aggregate usage statistics and crash reports. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide halal/haram analysis of food products
  • Improve our ingredient database and AI models
  • Fix bugs and enhance app performance
  • Respond to user feedback and reports

4. Data Storage

Your analysis history is stored locally on your device only. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on our servers. You can delete your local history at any time through the app settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Cloud Vision API for text extraction from images (OCR)
  • OpenAI API for ingredient classification - images are processed by OpenAI's servers and are subject to OpenAI's data retention policies
  • Google AdMob for advertisements (free users only)
  • RevenueCat for subscription management

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Advertising

Free users may see advertisements served by Google AdMob. These ads are configured to filter inappropriate content categories including alcohol, gambling, and dating. You can upgrade to Premium for an ad-free experience.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local analysis history
  • Request deletion of any reports you've submitted
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
